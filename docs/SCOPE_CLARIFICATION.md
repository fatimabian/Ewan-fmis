# FMIS Role and Screenshot Scope Clarification

FMIS implements two authenticated roles:

1. **Administrator** — manages user accounts, service catalogs, system settings, reports, and audit activity.
2. **Staff / AEW** — manages farmer registration, farm parcels, crops or commodities, service requests, operational reports, and field QR access.

Farmers are external stakeholders and service beneficiaries. They do not directly access FMIS and therefore do not have a Customer Dashboard. Any final-design requirement requesting Customer Dashboard screenshots should be marked **Not Applicable — no customer account role in the approved FMIS scope**, subject to written confirmation from the professor/client.

The final screenshot set should substitute farmer-facing service evidence, such as the staff-managed farmer profile, registration confirmation, or printable secure QR card, only after the professor approves the substitution.
